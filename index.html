About

We are a group who want to teach Mushin how to code.

  • May 2018

    The Idea is Born

    Our project lead, Sodiq had just finished the GLP program and was looking for a project to do. And the idea struck him: Why not teach people in your area how to code?!

  • June 1, 2018

    A Name is Born

    After a little thinking, we asked, why can't Mushin win with code?

  • August 20, 2018

    The first phase

    We had our first twelve students who applied to different tracks of the program and we began with them. They were able to host their projects on github as they learnt the fundamentals of github to help them on their journey to becoming developers. They were impressive and we are happy to have been involved with them.
